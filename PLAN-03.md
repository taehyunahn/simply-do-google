# PLAN-03: Brand Story Integration Strategy

## 1. Objective
To infuse the narrative depth and strategic positioning from `BrandStory.md` into the existing `index.html`. The goal is to shift the tone from "Functional Utility" (we make sheets) to "Business Liberation" (we free you from drudgery and subscription fatigue), while maintaining the clean, modern visual structure the user likes.

## 2. Guiding Principles
*   **No Structural Changes:** Keep the current HTML layout (Hero, Grid, Sections). Only update text content.
*   **Keywords:** Micro-ERP, Ownership (No Subscription), Cognitive Fluency (Zero Learning Curve), Liberation.
*   **Tone:** Professional, empathetic, confident (Social Proof), and slightly philosophical (Seth Godin vibe).

## 3. Detailed Content Updates

### A. Hero Section (`<header>`)
*   **Current Headline:** "반복되는 업무를 [Typing] 방식으로 해결합니다"
*   **Strategic Update:** Maintain the typing effect but strengthen the static text to reflect the "Micro-ERP" and "Liberation" concept.
*   **Proposed Subtext:**
    *   *From:* "새로운 기술을 배울 필요 없습니다..."
    *   *To:* "매달 나가는 구독료, 배우기 힘든 복잡한 툴은 이제 그만. **당신의 비즈니스를 위한 '영구 소유형 마이크로 ERP'**를 구축하세요."
